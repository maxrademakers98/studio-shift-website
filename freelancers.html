Who we're looking for

The profiles
we work with.

Our projects span strategy, design, and implementation. We look for depth over breadth — specialists who are genuinely expert in their domain.

Strategy

Sustainability Strategists

Experts in ESG frameworks, transition roadmaps, and materiality assessments. You help companies set credible targets and build the plans to meet them.

Design

Circular Design Specialists

Designers with deep knowledge of circular economy principles, material flows, and product end-of-life. You design for longevity, repairability, and reuse.

Supply Chain

Supply Chain Experts

Specialists in Scope 3 mapping, supplier engagement, and responsible sourcing. You know how to trace impact across complex global value chains.

Measurement

LCA & Reporting Analysts

Practitioners in life cycle assessment, carbon accounting, and sustainability reporting (GRI, CSRD, TCFD). You turn data into credible, defensible claims.

Innovation

Material & Process Innovators

Experts in alternative materials, bio-based inputs, or manufacturing process optimisation. You bridge the gap between R&D and real-world implementation.
